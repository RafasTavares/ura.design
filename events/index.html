Job Description

Ura is looking for a Frontend Developer working primarily with ReactJS to develop and maintain Identihub, our self-hosted brand and asset management software licensed under the free and open source AGPL license. Identihub makes hosting and reusing your project’s visual assets easy by automating image conversions and offering advanced features for handling SVG files, colors, fonts and images. You can find more about it at: https://identihub.co

Essential Responsibilities (including but not limited to)

  • Work together with our Backend Developer to develop new features
  • Assist with debugging / development of bug fixes. Consistent maintenance
  • Work in an open source environment (Mattermost, Etherpad, GitHub, Nextcloud)
  • 5-20h / week remote work (flexible regarding time commitments)

Required:

  • Excellent written and verbal English skills
  • 1+ years experience working with ReactJS
  • Basic Linux admin (ability to SSH, view logs, run tests)
  • Understanding of Git version control and developing in the open
  • Understanding of local development environments and integration of varying components

Desired:

  • Excellent written and verbal Albanian skills
  • Experience with Laravel and PHP

Payment is done on an hourly basis. Please let us know of your desired hourly rate so we can discuss payment terms based on your experience.
